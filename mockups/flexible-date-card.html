Flexible-date trip card

When a trip is built with flexible dates, the stored start/end span the whole availability window (e.g. "anytime Jan–May") while the duration badge holds the real trip length. Today the card prints the literal window next to the badge, which reads like a bug.

Detection (no schema change): a trip is treated as flexible when the stored date span is much larger than its length — (end − start + 1) > trip_length + 3. Real example: Tampa stores Jan 1 – May 31 (150 days) with trip_length = 10.
