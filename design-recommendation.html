✓ This direction works because…
→Dark sidebar anchors the layout and makes the content area feel warm and inviting
→Parchment background gives the "journal" warmth — clinical white doesn't
→White cards with shadows lift naturally off the parchment — strong contrast
→Cormorant on the big h1/h2 titles makes a real editorial statement — not hidden in tiny labels
→Stat values in Cormorant (the big numbers) look elegant and magazine-like
✗ The current version struggles because…
→White bg + parchment cards = very low contrast, cards don't pop off the background
→Parchment sidebar feels disconnected and "unanchored"
→Cormorant is wasted as a tiny 12px overline label — nobody notices it
→Everything has the same visual weight — no clear hierarchy between bg, cards, and content
Mockup only — no changes made to the app yet.
